My child has already taken some art classes. Can they still take this course?

Absolutely! Art, Animation & Video is much more than a traditional art class. It's multidisciplinary and innovative, combining the best of analog and digital practices. Do I need any special setup/stationery to enroll in the course?

No. We will send you a Digital CamKit containing an Art-Cam and all required art supplies from our partner, Faber Castell.

Yes! All our Art, Animation & Video classes are 1-to-1. This means that your child’s dedicated teacher will give your child their full attention.

We recommend 2 classes a week.
