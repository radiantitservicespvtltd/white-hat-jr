Frequently Asked Question's(FAQs)

Can children learn a musical instrument online?

Absolutely! We do three things to make that happen: (1) use a custom-built curriculum created especially for an online class, (2) rigorously train teachers to deliver delightful online classes, (3) use a custom platform for music that supports resources like backing tracks and sheet music.


Do I need an instrument to enroll in the course?

No. You do not need an instrument for the trial or for the first four classes of the Discovery level.


Which instruments do you teach in this course?

Piano and guitar.


My child has taken some classes already. Will they start as a beginner?

During the trial class, our teacher will assess your child and discuss the right starting point for them with you.


Can we skip the Discovery level and start instrument classes directly?

Yes you can. However, we encourage students to start with the Discovery level, as it builds excitement for learning music which makes for a great start to their learning journey!


Do students learn Western classical music or something else?

Students are most motivated when they learn and play songs they like. The songs may be from any genre, including Western classical music. We recognize that Western classical music builds a strong foundation, so we incorporate its techniques and offer many choices from legends like Mozart, Beethoven and Bach to our students.
