Frequently Asked Question's(FAQs)

Will this course be helpful for academics?

Our pathbreaking curriculum is designed based on Leading Edge Research that recommends inculcating a Math Mindset in children through projects and play. This helps children enjoy the subject and build Math confidence for life.


How is this course different from the one taught in schools?

Math is commonly taught in schools in an abstract form, which does not help children build a connection with the subject. The conventional exam process adds to the Math anxiety among children. To address these gaps, our pathbreaking curriculum is built on the real-world application of Math. It is designed to help your child understand the “Why” behind Math, build a strong foundation of concepts, solve complex problems, create something meaningful, and therefore, learn Math at an advanced pace.


What is taught in this course?

We teach Math concepts connecting them to real-world occurrences through the unique Capstone Projects. Our pathbreaking curriculum follows the US Common Core Standards and is adapted to ICSE and CBSE boards for Indian students.


Does this include the school Math portion?

The curriculum is adapted for ICSE and CBSE board Math curriculum and is based on US Common Core Standards for Math.


Will these be one-on-one classes?

Yes. We offer one-on-one classes to ensure each student gets the personalized attention they need to excel at Math. The teacher first understands your child's learning level for each topic and accordingly introduces activities based on what's best suited for them.


What is the age eligibility?

Presently, we have classes for grades 1 to 8, but higher grades will be available soon.


What will be the benefits of this particular course?

Our curriculum is built as a series of activities on GeoGebra, which is a leading tech platform that helps children work on Math problems in an interactive and fun way. Our dedicated teachers are trained to celebrate mistakes and see them as a learning opportunity. Our unique Capstone Projects build Math modeling skills, integrate coding for computational purposes, and help build applications and solutions that can solve real-world problems.
